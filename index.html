Income Statement Analysis
- Power BI

One of my favorite topics to work on is analyzing, transforming, and modeling financial data. The fun part of this project was using field parameters with slicers to implement dynamic currency conversion (US Dollar, Euro, and UK Pound) and a numeric parameter for a dynamic tax rate on the dashboard. The purpose of this project is to provide insights into the performance of RED30 Tech over the last three years (2022 - 2024). Specifically, the project addresses the following questions:
1. Is the company making a profit?
2. Is the company sticking to its budgeting plan?
3. During which period of the year is the company over-budgeting, and how does that affect their income?
